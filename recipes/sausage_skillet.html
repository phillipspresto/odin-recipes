Ingredients

  • Brown Rice
  • Smoked Turkey Sausage
  • Red, Green, or Yellow Bell Peppers
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Balsamic Vinegar
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • Parmesan Cheese

  1. Cook brown rice in instant pot
  2. Brown the sausage in a skillet
  3. Add peppers, onions, and garlic
  4. Add the rice
  5. add Balsamic Vinager and Red Pepper Flakes
  6. Top with Parmesan to serve
