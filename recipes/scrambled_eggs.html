Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • milk
  • American Cheese
  • White bread toast

Steps

  1. Crack eggs into skillet and scramble
  2. Mix in a splash of milk
  3. Once the eggs begin to solidify, mix in the American cheese
  4. While eggs are cooking, toast the white bread and butter to satisfaction
  5. As eggs are fully cooked, remove from pan, put on plate with toast, and enjoy!
